About Me

I am Bhaskar Ghosh, a PhD Candidate at the Computer Science Department of University of Lousiaina at Lafayette. I am a Research Assistant working under Dr. Khalid Elgazzar. I work with Computer Vision. I am currently trying to use Machine Learning for event detection and classification from video feeds. I plan to use this work in a Smart City environemnt. I have also been part of research being done on using Machine Learning to Edge Computing and Hardware Solutions to the Internet of Things.

Before this, I have completed my MS in Computer Science at University of Lousiana at Lafayette. I have worked on projects dealing with Energy Efficient solutions for Wearable Devices and Traffic Monitoring Systems. I plan to use my works in making the earth a better place!
